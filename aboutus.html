About

Brent grew up in the small town of Paintsville, Kentucky nestled in the heart of the Appalachian Mountains. From a young age, Brent was fascinated with all things food related and those who cooked it. Whether it be his mother, grandmother or a chef on television, he was amazed to watch them turn simple ingredients into amazing, flavorful food. However, being from a small town, cooking for a living never was a viable option. So, Brent followed other interests, attended Western Kentucky University, but never lost his passion for bringing people together around the table.

Brent started culinary school in 2007 and started at his first professional culinary job the same year. He has worked in hotels, restaurants, country clubs and most recently, was the Executive Chef for a large scale catering company in Nashville, Tennessee. This diversity of experience has led Brent to develop a unique culinary perspective and he has become well-versed in a variety of cuisines and techniques. Brent has also learned to scale this for 2 people or 10,000 people and deliver an amazing experience for those he serves.

Brent now wants to bring this wealth of knowledge and experience to clients on a more personal level and is focused on providing unique and intimate gatherings to clients on a smaller scale.
