Cauliflower Mashed Potatoes

Ingredients:

  • 5 medium russet potatoes
  • 1 head of cauliflower
  • 1 stick of butter
  • 1 cup of heavy cream
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp pepper
  • Optional minced parsley for garnish

Instructions:

Clean and peel potatoes. Cut Cauliflower into florets. In separate medium pots, boil each until they are fork tender.

In a small pot, bring butter and cream to a heavy simmer until the butter is fully melted and incorporated.

In a large bowl, blend the potatoes, cauliflower, cream mixture, salt and pepper to desired consistency. Chef Brent prefers the mash to have a few lumps remaining.

Scoop mash into your serving bowl and garnish with minced parsley.

Chocolate Avocado Mousse

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 4 very ripe avocados
  • 1/2 cup agave (or maple syrup)
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tbsp pure vanilla extract
  • 1/4 tsp salt

Instructions:

Place the chocolate chips in a small bowl. Place over a small saucepan of barely simmering water. Stir until the chocolate is melted and smooth, about 3 minutes. Set aside to cool slightly.

Place the melted chocolate, avocados, agave, cocoa powder, vanilla and salt in a food processor. Blend until smooth and creamy, scraping the sides of the bowl as needed. Spoon into glasses and refrigerate for at least 3 hours.
